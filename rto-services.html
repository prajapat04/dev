RTO Bangalore Online

RTO Bangalore comes under Transport department karnataka. RTO office Bangalore is designated for traffic regulations, Road tax collection, issue of Driving license, vehicle registration and other associated works such as transfer of ownership, HP addition / termination, NOC etc. Road tax in Bangalore is to be paid at RTO Bangalore.

To manage the administrative work RTO Bangalore has 8 RTO office in Bangalore area wise namely Bangalore South RTO, Bangalore West RTO, Bangalore North RTO, RTO Bangalore East Kasturi Nagar, RTO Indiranagar Bangalore, RTO Jayanagar Bangalore, RTO Central Bangalore.

Frequently Asked Questions

I have been shifted to Pune from Bangalore . I have Bangalore registered car . Is vehicle presence required at Bangalore RTO to get NOC ? Do I need to visit RTO Bangalore for this ?
Most payments reflect on the official portal within 24-48 working hours. However, you receive a confirmation receipt from Itzeazy immediately after the transaction is successful.
Will my vehicle’s registration in the Bangalore RTO be valid if I obtain NOC to move my vehicle from one state to another but do not complete the process of re-registration?
Vehicle's registration in original RTO is not valid, once NOC for the vehicle is issued. You can not approach RTO for any service like duplicate RC, fitness, ownership transfer. Its advisable to re-register the vehicle to new RTO as soon as possible. If RC or NOC gets lost in between , its very complicated scenario
Do I need to have my mobile number linked to my driving licence for this process?
Yes, you must link your mobile number with your driving licence. You will receive OTP on your registered mobile number. You need this OTP to verify your identity during process.
I have purchased a used car in Bangalore . I have only rent agreement as address proof of mine in Bangalore . Will the rent agreement will work as address proof ?
yes, In Bangalore rent agreement is acceptable as address proof for transfer of ownership of the vehicle.
Is Digilocker RC valid for vehicle transfer?
Original RC is required for RC transfer in RTO Bangalore. Digilocker RC is not valid for vehicle ownership transfer. If you have lost original RC , then you should apply for duplicate RC first.
I have lost RC of my bike which is registered in Bangalore . Can I apply for duplicate RC online ?
As of now the Duplicate RC process is not online in Bangalore. Application for duplicate RC along with supporting documents should be submitted physically to the concerned RTO . Again after work completion, RC is to be received by person only from the RTO. However , Itzeazy has online process for duplicate RC in Bangalore . Its hassle free door step service. In our process you need to visit only once for signature verification purpose that too only for half an hour. Rest of the process is managed by Itzeazy team.
My car was registered in Bangalore RTO . I have lost RC of my car. The problem is I have been shifted to Mysore. What should I do ? How I will get duplicate RC ? Do I need to take CC ?
Yes, Its possible to apply for duplicate RC and fitness together . Along with submitting necessary documents for duplicate RC and fitness process in the RTO , once visit of registered owner to the RTO and inspection of vehicle is mandatory. For detail process related guide you may contact our team.
My vehicle was on loan which I have already closed 3 years back . Bank had given NOC for loan closure which expired long back . Now I want to go for removal of Hypothecation from vehicle in the RTO. Do I need to take fresh NOC from the bank?
It will be 3 stage process :
1- Duplicate RC in Bangalore
2- CC from Bangalore to Mysore
3- Change of address in Mysore
When address gets changed within Karnataka and new address falls in different state, CC is required to change the address on RC.
