Mock test for Learning License

If you have applied for a learner’s license, you need to prepare yourself for RTO exam. Test for learning licence check your theoretical knowledge about road safety and traffic rules. In the learners test or LL test , there are 15 multiple choice questions asked . You need to answer 9 questions correctly to clear the test. You get 30 seconds to answer each question.

Guidelines for Attempting Driving Licence Test


You should know few things before taking the RTO exam test:

• Be prepared, know the basics of driving and road signs before appearing for your test
• Practice through mock tests for learning license
• Arrive at the RTO centre half an hour before the test
• Carry all the necessary documents with you to the test centre

Now you don’t need to go to the RTO office for giving the LLR test. You can take the learner’s licence test online from home. Many state governments have implemented this option. The procedure for application would be the same.
