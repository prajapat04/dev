Vehicle owner details by number plate

The national vehicle registry contains information of any related or vehicle registrations of motor vehicles, bikes, and cars in India. It compiles all the information on driving licenses and vehicle registration details. RTO offices in India possess the date of RC and vehicle related information.

  • Buying a second-hand vehicle to verify legal ownership history.
  • Hit and run / investigation to track down the responsible parties.
  • Verifying insurance like PUC (Pollution Under Control) validity.
  • Legal proceedings where someone's documentation is required by law.
  • Prevention of fraud and unauthorised vehicle transfers.

How to check vehicle owner name by vehicle number

You can find the vehicle owner name by vehicle number online using two methods.

Vehicle details using VAHAN website
  1. 1 Visit the official Vahan MII portal at vahan.nic.in
  2. 2 Navigate to the 'Know Your Vehicle Details' tab on the homepage.
  3. 3 Create an account or login using your registered mobile number.
  4. 4 Enter the complete vehicle number without spaces or dashes.
  5. 5 Complete the verification captcha for security.
  6. 6 Click on 'Search Vehicle' to fetch real-time data.
  7. 7 Review the owner name, RC status, model, and fuel type displayed.

Vehicle number plate format

AB
State Code (AB)

The first two alphabets indicate the state or Union territory of India. For example, vehicle registered in Maharashtra starts with the code 'MH' whereas in Delhi it is 'DL'. The two most significant alphabets of the state's name is used.

07
District Code (07)

The next two digits refer to district's sequential number. Every state in India has at least 1 district in it, the district RTO itself manages the registration of new vehicles. Each district in India has its own RTO, which is in charge of the drivers and vehicle registrations.

CD3456
Unique Identity
(CD3456)

The last two alphabets and digits of the vehicle's license plate is unique number which helps to identify the vehicle. If number is unavailable, then alphabets are used to replace the last digit. This guarantees an excess number of codes for all vehicles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I check two wheeler owner details online?
Yes, you can check two wheeler owner details online using the VAHAN portal or ItzeEazy. Simply enter the vehicle registration number to get the owner name, RC status, and other details.
Are there any limits on registration lookups?
The VAHAN portal may have daily limits on the number of lookups per user. However, using ItzeEazy you can perform multiple lookups without restrictions.
Can I check chassis number via number plate?
Yes, by entering the vehicle number plate you can retrieve the chassis number from the RC details. The last 5 digits of the chassis number are usually visible in the search results.
Will I get the full name of the owner?
Due to privacy regulations, only the initials of the owner's name are displayed publicly. Full name disclosure requires proper legal authorization.
Can I find details for very old vehicles?
Details for vehicles registered before 2000 may not be available digitally as older records were maintained manually. Vehicles registered after 2000 are generally available in the VAHAN database.
