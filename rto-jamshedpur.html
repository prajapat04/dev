RTO Jamshepur Online

RTO Jamshedpur comes under transport department of Jharkhand. DTO Jamshedpur is responsible for traffic regulations, Road tax collection, issue of Driving liecnce,vehicle registration and other associated works such as transfer of ownership, HP addition/termination, NOC etc. in East Singhbhum district of Jharkhand. To manage the administrative works in Jamshedpur there is one district transport office i.e. DTO Jamshedpur (JH07).

RTO Jamshedpur performs different tasks or activities required to ensure safe and appropriate transport on the road. RTO Jamshedpur is primarily responsible for the enforcement of the rules and regulations in road transport in the Jamshedpur region.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it required to go on different dates for driving tests for different classes of vehicle?
Yes, you have to go if you have booked an appointment for a driving test.
Do the bike or two wheeler vehicle require fitness certificate from RTO Jamshedpur?
Yes, the fitness certificate is issued for 15 years at the time of registration and then again it is renewed further.
How to re register vehicle after 15 years in Jamshedpur?
After 15 years of completion, it needs to re register vehicle by submitting Form 25 and other required documents at respective RTO in Jharkhand.
When can we get temporary registration in Jamshedpur?
If the vehicle owner wants to take his vehicle to another state, then only a temporary registration certificate is issued.
Can we make any changes in the basic structure of any vehicle?
No, basic structure of any vehicle is not allowed to be changed except engine modified as per fuel type.
How long is a vehicle's temporary registration valid?
Temporary registration of a vehicle is valid for one month or 30 days, which is mainly issued when the owner of a vehicle wants to take his vehicle to another state.
