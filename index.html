Traffic Challan in India

Vehicle drivers and owners have to follow the traffic rules set by the transport department of India under Motor vehicle act. The rules are for the safety of the people and maintaining organised road transportation. Violation of traffic rules may lead to penalty and severe punishment in India.

What is traffic E Challan

E challan is the modern CCTV enabled auto generated challan system to reduce the number of road accidents in India. The CCTV camera records braking of traffic rules on road and generate e challan for the offence. Traffic challan system is linked to the vehicle registration number or driving licence number. Thus, you will get notified through SMS. You can pay the fine online using UPI, debit card, credit card, or net banking.

How to pay challan

Here is how to pay challan online via official website of state transport:

  1. 1

    Visit your state's official transport website.

  2. 2

    You will find a section dedicated to pay violation fines.

  3. 3

    You can choose to pay for traffic violations, parking charges, spot fines, or other charges.

  4. 4

    Click the appropriate link and enter the required details, which will vary depending on the type of violation.

  5. 5

    Enter your new vehicle registration number, old registration number, or parking violation tag number.

  6. 6

    Enter the details and the current outstanding amount.

  7. 7

    You can pay with your Mastercard or Visa card.

    MASTERCARD
    VISA
  8. 8

    Do not forget to enter the captcha in the space provided and before clicking on 'submit'

  9. 9

    You will be redirected to a safe page to make your payment
